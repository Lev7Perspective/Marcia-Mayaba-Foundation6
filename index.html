About the Foundation

Honoring legacy through action by creating opportunities for the girl child

The Marcia Mayaba Foundation exists to honor legacy through action by creating opportunities for the girl child from early development to adulthood, and opening pathways into education and industries, in particular the automotive sector.

Guided by grace, dignity, and strength, we empower girls and women to break barriers and achieve their full potential in traditionally male-dominated industries.

Our comprehensive approach includes mentorship programs, educational support, industry exposure, and leadership development to create lasting change in our communities.

Our Programs

Comprehensive support from early development to career success

Mentorship Programs

One-on-one guidance and support for young women pursuing careers in the automotive industry, connecting them with successful professionals.

Education Support

Scholarships, tutoring, and resources to help girls excel academically and pursue higher education in STEM fields.

Industry Exposure

Workshops, factory tours, internships, and hands-on experience in the automotive sector to provide real-world insights.

Leadership Development

Training programs to build confidence, communication skills, and leadership capabilities for future industry leaders.
