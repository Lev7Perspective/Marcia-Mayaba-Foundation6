About the Foundation

The Marcia Mayaba Foundation exists to honour legacy through action by creating opportunities for the girl child from early development to adulthood, and opening pathways into education and industries, in particular the automotive sector.

Guided by grace, dignity, and strength, we empower girls and women to break barriers and achieve their full potential.

Our mission is to create a world where every girl has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of her background or circumstances.

Our Programs

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance and support for young women pursuing careers in the automotive industry.

Education Support

Scholarships, tutoring, and resources to help girls excel academically and pursue higher education.

Industry Exposure

Workshops, factory tours, and internships in the automotive sector to provide real-world experience.

Leadership Development

Training programs to build confidence, communication skills, and leadership capabilities.
